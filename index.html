Hello, my name is Lisa and I want to craft beautiful experiences of all sorts for people of all sorts. While I love the vast variety of work within the design field, I am especially passionate about visual design, interaction design, data visualization, and front-end development.

I am currently pursuing a bachelor of fine arts in Graphic Design, with a Computer Science and Informatics double minor at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Fuse is a mass photosharing phone application. You can create an album, add your friends to the album and everyone in the group will have access to pictures taken by anyone in the group. You therefore never have to send or wait to be sent photos.

I designed the application, and it is currenly being developed by Jason Su. Coming to the Google Play store soon.

Designed the logo, custom patterned wrapping paper, and gift box for a soap kit. The gift item was to be based on a critter in the park; I chose the bee. Bees release a pheromone to call to other bees when they are stressed. In order to reflect this behavior, I chose to create a soap kit, which relaxes one through scents (which are made with bee products).
